DMW manufactures telescopic stiff boom cranes for light handling requirements but with robust designs including dual rack and pinion rotation. Available for use on any vessel the DMW SMALL MARINE cranes offer light in weight but with variable capacities and outreachs up to 255,400 ft/lbs lifting capacity. This product is excellent for use in oil skimming operations - for handling skimmers, booms and hoses.
